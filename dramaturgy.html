DRAMATURGY

“My dramaturgy practice has never been interested in borders. I move between production, creative consulting for Broadway playwrights and screenwriters, literary review boards, dance concerts, film and web series, and panel curation, following a project’s questions wherever they lead. I bring the same rigor to a script in development as I do to a selection committee, a research archive, or a dance rehearsal. The throughline is always a curiosity about how meaning is made, by whom, and through what means. While much of this work must remain confidential, below are a couple of public examples.”
